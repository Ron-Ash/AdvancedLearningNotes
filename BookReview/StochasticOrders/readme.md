# Stochastic Orders
The following are my notes from reading the 2007 book "***Stochastic Orders***" by ***Moshe Shaked*** and ***J. George Shanthikumar***.
- link to book: https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-0-387-34675-5

*Stochastic ordering* is a fundamental guide for decision making under uncertainty, and is an essential tool in the study of structural properties of complex stochastic systems.
